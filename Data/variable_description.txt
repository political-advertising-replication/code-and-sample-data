WOM_DATA.csv
CAN_WOM_5_POST: number of Tweets about candidate within 5 minutes after the ad airs (disguised)
CAN_WOM_5_PRE: number of Tweets about candidate within 5 minutes before the ad airs (disguised)
DEM: 1 if the ad supports Clinton and 0 otherwise
ATT: 1 if attack/negative tone and 0 otherwise
AD_LENGTH_IND: 1 if an ad is over 30 seconds and 0 otherwise
AD_POSITION: relative ad position within ad break (bounded from 0 and 1)
AUDIENCE: number of viewers for the ad at the beginning of the ad
slant: measure of extremeness
consistency: measure of consistent messaging
WEEK: week indicator
Day: day of the week indicator
TIME_WINDOW: time of the day indicator in the prime TV time (30-minute increment)
PROG_GENRE: program type as labeled by Kantar Media Stradegy
NETWORK: network in which the ad airs
PRE_OCT_1: 1 if the ad airs prior to Oct. 1 and 0 otherwise
POST_OCT_1: 1 if the ad airs on or after Oct. 1 and 0 otherwise
AD_CREATIVE_ID: unique ad creative ID provided from Kantar Media Stradegy (disguised)

VOTER_PREFERENCE_DATA.csv
DEM: 1 if the ad supports Clinton and 0 otherwise
WEEK: week indicator
Vote_Share: voter preference in % (disguised)
Lagged_VS: one day lagged voter preference in % (disguised)
ad_day: 1 if the candidate aired ads in the previous day and 0 otherwise
no_ad_day: 1 if the candidate did not air ads in the previous day and 0 otherwise
before_oct: 1 if the ad airs prior to Oct. 1 and 0 otherwise
after_oct: 1 if the ad airs on or after Oct. 1 and 0 otherwise
TOT_INSTANCE: number of ad airings in the previous day
POSITION: weighted average of AD_POSITION in the previous day
LENGTH: weighted average of AD_LENGTH_IND in the previous day
tot_audience: summation of the AUDIENCE variable in the previous day
comp_audience: summation of the AUDIENCE variable of the rival candidate in the previous day
Weighted_Slant: weighted average of of the slant variable in the previous day
Weighted_Cosine: weighted average of of the consistency variable in the previous day
NEG: weighted average of of the ATT variable in the previous day
AWARDS: share of total number of viewers on the awards program
COMEDY: share of total number of viewers on the comedy program
DOCUMENTARY: share of total number of viewers on the documentary program
DRAMA_ADVENTURE: share of total number of viewers on the drame/adventure program
MOVIE: share of total number of viewers on the movie program
NEWS_POLITICAL: share of total number of viewers on the news program
SLICE_OF_LIFE: share of total number of viewers on the slide of life program
SPORTS: share of total number of viewers on the sports program
SUSPENSE_MYSTERY: share of total number of viewers on the suspense/mystery program
ANE: share of total number of viewers on the ANE network
CNN: share of total number of viewers on the CNN network
DISC: share of total number of viewers on the DISC network
FNEW: share of total number of viewers on the FNEW network
HIST: share of total number of viewers on the HIST network
ID: share of total number of viewers on the ID network
LIFE: share of total number of viewers on the LIFE network
MSNB: share of total number of viewers on the MSNB network
NBC: share of total number of viewers on the NBC network
OTHERS: share of total number of viewers on the OTHERS network
TLC: share of total number of viewers on the TLC network
row_num: row number
one: a column of 1's
